# Module 6: Human-Robot Interaction

This module explores the complex and important field of human-robot interaction (HRI). As robots become more prevalent in our daily lives, their ability to communicate, collaborate, and coexist with humans becomes increasingly important. This module covers the social, ethical, and technical aspects of human-robot interaction.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will:

- Understand the principles of effective human-robot interaction
- Know the ethical considerations in robotics
- Be familiar with safety protocols for human-robot collaboration
- Appreciate the social implications of robotics technology

## Module Overview

This module covers:

1. **Social Robotics** - Designing robots that interact naturally with humans
2. **Safety and Ethics** - Ensuring responsible and safe robot deployment

These topics are crucial for the successful integration of robots into human spaces and society.
